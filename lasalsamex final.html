Our Story

Real Mexican Flavor,
Right Here in Carrollton

At La Salsa Mex, we bring the bold, vibrant flavors of Mexico straight to your table. From sizzling fajitas and hand-rolled enchiladas to fresh ceviches and hearty combination plates — every dish is made with love and authentic recipes.

Whether you're joining us for a weekday lunch special or a family dinner, we promise a warm welcome and unforgettable food every single time.
